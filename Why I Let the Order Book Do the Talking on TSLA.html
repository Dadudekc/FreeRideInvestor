Why I Let the Order Book Do the Talking on TSLA

1. Intro: The Language of Big Money

Candles and indicators show what happened. The order book tells you what’s about to happen. If you’re not reading it, you’re missing out on TSLA’s biggest clues.

2. Why the Order Book Works

  • Big Sell Walls → resistance zones.
  • Big Buy Walls → support levels.
  • It’s real-time supply and demand—not lagging indicators.

3. How I Trade With the Order Book

Example: Sell Wall at $475.60

  • 2,064 shares stacked → sellers defending hard.
  • What I Did: Shorted at $475.50 when volume stalled.
  • Result: Price reversed cleanly to $473.

Example: Buy Wall at $470.00

  • 3,000 shares stacked → buyers stepping up.
  • What I Did: Went long at $470.10 → Targeted $472.

4. Combining Order Book With Indicators

  • VWAP: Buy walls near VWAP = high-probability longs.
  • RSI: Oversold + big size on bids = bounce trade.

5. Key Takeaways

  • Stop guessing. Start reading. The order book shows you where the big players are hiding.
  • Spot big size → build your trades around it.
