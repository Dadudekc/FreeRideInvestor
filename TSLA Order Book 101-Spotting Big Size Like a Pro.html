TSLA Order Book 101: Spotting Big Size Like a Pro

1. Intro: Big Players, Big Moves

Trading TSLA? The order book is your secret weapon. It’s where the whales leave their footprints—big sizes at key levels can stop price in its tracks or send it flying. The trick? Learning to spot “big size” in the noise.

2. What Qualifies as Big Size for TSLA?

  • Small size: 10–500 shares → typical noise.
  • Medium size: 500–1,000 shares → worth watching near levels.
  • Big size: 1,000–5,000 shares → actionable signals.
  • Major size: 5,000+ shares → whales are in play.

5. Why Big Size Matters

  • Large orders = big players.
  • Big bids attract → support.
  • Big asks repel → resistance.

6. Key Takeaway

For TSLA, 1,000+ shares is where the game changes. Learn to spot it, trade it, and let the whales do the heavy lifting.
